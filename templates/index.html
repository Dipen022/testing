OBJECTIVE

The primary objective of this project is to create an intuitive and accessible web interface that empowers users to explore and understand the surfactant performance of various detergent samples. By integrating a database of detergent samples with their respective Critical Micelle Concentration (CMC) values, the webpage facilitates seamless selection and retrieval of pertinent data. This platform serves as a valuable resource for researchers, scientists, and industry professionals involved in the formulation, testing, and optimization of detergent products. Through transparent and efficient dissemination of surfactant data, the project aims to enhance decision-making processes and drive advancements in detergent technology.
